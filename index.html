Portfolio

3D Drag & Drop Game

The 3D Drag & Drop game is a simple but entertaining project that challenges players to catch bombs and drop them near a rabbit in order to knock it off a platform. The game requires players to be quick and precise in their movements, as the bombs pass quickly across the screen. Once the bombs are caught, players must strategically place them so that they fall close to the rabbit to cause enough force to knock it off the platform. The game's simplicity makes it accessible to players of all ages and skill levels, while its 3D graphics and physics-based gameplay add an element of excitement and challenge. Thanks to its intuitive drag-and-drop mechanics and unique gameplay, 3D Drag & Drop is a great choice for anyone looking for a fun and engaging gaming experience.

Virtual Reality Video Game

In this virtual reality video game, players are challenged to escape from a room without being caught by guards. The goal is to find a key to open the door and escape to the next level. However, guards constantly patrol the room and, if they detect the player, they will start chasing them. Players must navigate the room and avoid detection by the guards, using stealth and strategy to evade their pursuit. One of the special features of this game is that it is able to detect braking, this movement is programmed to be an alternative to the left joystick that is used to run or walk through the game, the game also detects when the player crouches, this modifies the player's in-game model in order to reflect that behavior.

Tower Defense Game

Tower Defense games are a popular genre of strategy games that require players to defend a specific location against waves of enemy attacks. In this game, players must defend their territory by placing different types of towers and strategically summoning ships to counter enemy waves. As the game progresses, the enemy AI becomes more aggressive, making it more difficult to defend against their attacks. To succeed in the game, the player must carefully plan and choose his ships, taking into account the abilities of each unit. In addition, the player must manage his resources efficiently, choosing when and with which unit to spend his resources to maximize his attack and defense capabilities. Tower Defense games are known for their addictive gameplay and challenging difficulty, making them a favorite of players of all ages.
